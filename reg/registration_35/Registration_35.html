Question : Why should it be important for EU to have a more integrated foreign policy ?
Answer :In modern day European Union the member countries are frequently divided in opinion concerning their respective foreign policies. This can lead to numerous problems that can hurt the European Union, the member countries and the countries outside European Union. The most obvious problem of individually different foreign policies is the possible disintegration and the diminution of the credit of European Union as a leading global coalition of countries. In global world of today the threats of international terrorism, numerous revolutions in Arab countries and war conflicts still remain important questions of today’s foreign politics. These problems concern not only the individual states, but the European Union as a whole. It is also important to note the different positions of the EU countries. These can cause problems in peaceful international relations not only outside the EU, but among its member states as well.
