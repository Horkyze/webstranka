Question : How can the V4 partnership be helpful in enlarging the EU by countries of Western Balcans and Eastern Partnership?
Answer :The V4-partnership not only shows that these countries have a record of international cooperation, but also that these countries are willing to convert goals and desires into actual sustainable results. The partnership leads as an example to former eastern-block countries on how to successfully integrate with the rest of Europe. It gave these countries a solid regional platform, through wich they could excert an influence far larger than they could have done on their own. The sheer fact that the Visegrad nations who already hold member status in the EU, are vouching for these countries should also be an advantage for the membership-seeking nations. The V4 partnership can also help these countries assert their national sovereignity, rather than becoming an extension of the influence of greater powers. To summarize it all, the V4-countries could act as a bridge between the EU and the remainder of the Eastern block countries.
