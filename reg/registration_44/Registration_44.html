Question : Greece supports the turkeys membership in EU although these two countries have had numerous disputes over their territory, for example the Cyprus dispute, that still isnt fully resolved . What benefits would Greece have from Turkeys EU membership?
Answer :Despite of many disputes between Turkey and Greece there are numerous benefits for Greece in case of Turkey joining the EU. Our delegation has chosen to name some of them mostly connected with the trade. Firstly, opening the borders of Greece for Turkey would attract Turkish people to work there because of Greek social system benefits that is above the average in Europe, therefore Greece could take adventage of cheaper labour force from Turkey. Secondly, Turkey joining the EU and possible accepting of EURO currency would mean no more expenses in trading caused by high exchange rate and paying custom duties. And also cheaper goods from Turkey could be available. Especially in clothing production. Another benefit would be security insurance in case of Cyprus dispute. These countries would be forced to maintain the status quo on border issues and another armed conflicts should be completely excluded and solved peacefully.
