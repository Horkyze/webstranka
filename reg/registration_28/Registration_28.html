Question : Why are countries like Slovakia still not willing to recognize Kosovo?
Answer :Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Slovak Republic takes note of the Kosovo unilateral declaration of indeopendance, but at the same time, Slovakia does not consider recognizing Kosovo on the basis of this declaration.
