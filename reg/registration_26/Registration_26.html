Question : Why should it be important for EU to have a more integrated foreign policy ?
Answer :In our opinion, a more integrated foreign policy will allow EU to influence international politics more effectively rather than every member of EU would try to act on its own. Now, we can often see that foreign policies of EU members are inconsequent or even when ideas are reasonable, there is a weak support for them. More integrated foreign policy will allow EU to be an equal partner to such countries as USA or China. Well- organised, efficient foreign policy might bring benefits to Eu countries. These are also economical benefits concerning international trade and using new technologies. This is also very important from the energetic point of view. Good example are Eu countries\' negotiations with Russia about gas transports. Better cooperation could allow to negotiate better financial conditions of such transactions which could decrease the price of gas in EU and automatically open the gate for faster economic development(industry strengthened) Financial crisis of last years showed that a lack of coordination and irrational, selfish decisions can lead to strong tensions around the world.Therefore, our group believes that integrated foreign policy of EU is one of the key factors leading to stronger, more respected Europe.
